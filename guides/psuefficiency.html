What is PSU Efficiency?

In a few words, a PSU is disigned to convert the AC power from your wall outlet to DC power that your other components can use.

The efficiency rating is simply the power outputted to those components divided by the wattage drawn from the wall socket.

A PSU of 1000W with a 50% efficiency rating would draw 2000W to get to that peak output.

In that case the other 1000W are wasted as heat in that conversion process.
